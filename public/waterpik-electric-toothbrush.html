Waterpik Electric Toothbrush Review: Combo Units and Sonic Brushes

Quick Answer: Waterpik is best known for water flossers, but their electric toothbrush lineup includes the innovative Sonic-Fusion (brush + flosser in one handle) and Complete Care combo systems (separate brush + countertop flosser). The combo approach makes sense for people who want to simplify their oral care routine. However, the toothbrush component alone doesn't match dedicated brands like Oral-B or Sonicare in cleaning power.

Waterpik's Electric Toothbrush Lineup

Waterpik's approach to electric toothbrushes is unique in the industry. Rather than competing directly with Oral-B and Sonicare on standalone brushes, Waterpik leverages their water flosser expertise to create integrated systems that combine brushing and flossing. This strategy makes sense given that Waterpik is the undisputed leader in water flossing technology.

The Sonic-Fusion: Brushing and Flossing Simultaneously

The Sonic-Fusion is Waterpik's most innovative product. It combines a sonic toothbrush with a water flosser in a single handle connected to a countertop base unit. Water flows through the brush handle and out through the brush head, allowing you to brush and floss at the same time. You can also use each function independently: brush only, floss only, or both together.

In practice, the simultaneous brushing and flossing experience takes some getting used to. The water flow during brushing can feel unusual at first, and there's more mess than either activity alone. Most users find that after a week of adjustment, the combined routine becomes natural and genuinely faster than brushing and flossing separately. A complete brush-and-floss session takes about 2-3 minutes versus 4-5 minutes doing them separately.

Toothbrush Quality Assessment

The honest assessment of Waterpik's toothbrush components is that they're adequate but not exceptional. Both the Sonic-Fusion brush and the Complete Care sonic brush deliver mid-range sonic cleaning that's better than manual brushing but noticeably less powerful than a Sonicare ProtectiveClean or Oral-B Pro 1000. The brush heads are functional but lack the refined bristle engineering of dedicated toothbrush brands.

This is an understandable trade-off. Waterpik's core competency is water flossing, and the toothbrush components serve as convenient additions to the system rather than standalone premium products. If your primary motivation is the water flosser and you want a decent sonic brush to go with it, Waterpik's combo systems deliver good value.

For most people, we recommend buying the best toothbrush you can afford from Oral-B or Sonicare and pairing it with a standalone Waterpik water flosser. This approach gives you premium-quality brushing plus Waterpik's excellent flossing technology. The combo units are best for people who prioritize counter space efficiency and routine simplicity over maximizing the quality of each individual component.

Pro Tip: If counter space is your primary concern, consider a Waterpik Cordless Advanced (portable water flosser) paired with any electric toothbrush. The cordless flosser stores in a drawer and takes up zero counter space, giving you the flexibility to choose the best toothbrush independently.

Who Should Buy Waterpik Electric Toothbrushes

Waterpik's electric toothbrush products make the most sense for people who are committed to water flossing and want a streamlined system. If you already own and love a Waterpik water flosser, upgrading to a Complete Care or Sonic-Fusion combo can simplify your routine. People with braces, bridges, or implants who need both thorough brushing and targeted water flossing will also benefit from the integrated approach.

Our Verdict

Waterpik's strength is water flossing, not toothbrush manufacturing. Their combo systems offer genuine convenience for people who want both functions, but the toothbrush performance is mid-range at best. For optimal dental care, pair a premium standalone electric toothbrush with a separate Waterpik flosser. However, the Complete Care 9.0 at 4.0/5 is a solid all-in-one choice for those who value simplicity.
