Costco Electric Toothbrush: Bundles, Deals, and Member Value

By ElectricToothbrush.site Editorial TeamUpdated: February 2026
Quick Summary

Costco consistently offers some of the best electric toothbrush deals available, especially on Oral-B and Sonicare bundles that include extra brush heads. Their exclusive bundles typically save 20-35% compared to buying the same items separately elsewhere. The 90-day return policy adds extra peace of mind.

Costco is one of the best places to buy an electric toothbrush if you are a member. The warehouse retailer negotiates exclusive bundle packages with Oral-B and Philips Sonicare that include extra brush heads, travel cases, and sometimes multiple handles for families. These bundles offer genuine savings that are difficult to beat, even during Amazon sales events.

This guide covers what is currently available at Costco, how to evaluate whether a bundle is actually a good deal, and when you might find better pricing elsewhere.

What Electric Toothbrushes Does Costco Sell?

Costco's selection focuses on the two biggest brands in the market. Here is what you typically find:

Oral-B at Costco

  • Oral-B iO Series bundles (Series 5, 6, or 9 depending on season) with extra brush heads and travel case
  • Oral-B Pro/Smart Series 2-pack for couples or families, often with 4-6 brush heads included
  • Oral-B replacement brush head bulk packs (8-10 heads per pack)

Philips Sonicare at Costco

  • Sonicare DiamondClean or ExpertClean bundles with extra heads and premium travel case
  • Sonicare ProtectiveClean 2-pack ideal for couples
  • Sonicare replacement head bulk packs (6-8 heads per pack)

Waterpik at Costco

  • Waterpik water flosser bundles sometimes combined with electric toothbrush handles
  • Waterpik Sonic-Fusion (toothbrush and water flosser combo) occasionally in stock
Costco Inventory Note

Costco's electric toothbrush inventory rotates throughout the year. Specific models and bundles change seasonally. What you see in-store may differ from what is available on Costco.com. Holiday season (November-December) typically has the widest selection and deepest discounts.

When Costco Wins

  • You want a bundle with extra heads (almost always cheaper at Costco)
  • You need two handles for a household (2-packs are outstanding value)
  • You value the extended 90-day return window
  • The specific model you want is currently stocked

When Amazon Wins

  • You want a specific model not available at Costco
  • Amazon is running a major sale (Prime Day, Black Friday)
  • You want budget brands like Fairywill or Burst (not sold at Costco)
  • Costco is out of stock on the model you want

Replacement Heads at Costco

Beyond the initial toothbrush purchase, Costco is one of the best places to buy replacement brush heads in bulk. Their multi-packs typically include 8-10 heads, which represents a full year's supply or more at the recommended 3-month replacement interval.

Typical Costco replacement head pricing:

  • Oral-B CrossAction 8-pack: $30-35 ($3.75-4.40 per head vs. $6-8 retail)
  • Sonicare Optimal Plaque Control 6-pack: $35-40 ($5.80-6.70 per head vs. $8-10 retail)
  • Oral-B iO Series heads 6-pack: $45-50 ($7.50-8.30 per head vs. $10-12 retail)

Tips for Shopping Electric Toothbrushes at Costco

  1. Check Costco.com first. Online pricing sometimes differs from in-store, and online-only deals are common.
  2. Watch for monthly coupon books. Costco's monthly member coupons frequently include electric toothbrush discounts of $20-40 off.
  3. Holiday timing matters. The best selection and prices appear November through January.
  4. Compare before buying. Use Amazon price trackers to confirm the Costco bundle is actually saving money.
  5. Stock up on heads. Even if you buy your brush elsewhere, Costco's bulk replacement head packs are almost always the best per-unit price.
