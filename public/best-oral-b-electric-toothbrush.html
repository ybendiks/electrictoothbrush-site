Oral-B Lineup Buying Guide

Understanding the Oral-B Product Lines

Oral-B currently sells two main product lines: the iO Series (iO Series 4, 5, 7, 9) which uses newer micro-vibration technology, and the Pro/Vitality Series (Pro 1000, Vitality) which uses traditional oscillating-rotating technology. Both lines are effective; the iO models deliver a smoother, quieter brushing experience.

iO vs Pro: Which Line Should You Choose?

The iO line represents Oral-B's latest technology with micro-vibrations that deliver energy to each bristle tip, magnetic charging, and interactive displays. The Pro line uses the older but still effective oscillating-rotating-pulsating system. If budget is no concern, go iO. If you want the best cleaning per dollar, the Pro 1000 is hard to beat. The cleaning difference between the two lines is measurable but modest.

Which iO Model Is Right for You?

All iO models share the same fundamental motor and brush head, so cleaning performance is identical. You are paying for additional modes, a better display, AI features, and color options. The Series 5 hits the sweet spot for most users. Choose the Series 9 only if you want AI tracking and the full mode selection. The Series 4 is fine if you just want the iO experience at the lowest cost.

Brush Head Compatibility

One important distinction: iO brush heads are not compatible with Pro/Vitality handles, and vice versa. The iO line uses a proprietary magnetic attachment system, while the Pro/Vitality line uses the classic click-on system. iO replacement heads are more expensive at roughly $10 each compared to $5-7 for classic heads. Factor this ongoing cost into your purchasing decision.

Our Verdict

For most people, the Oral-B iO Series 5 offers the ideal combination of flagship cleaning technology and reasonable pricing. It delivers the same core cleaning as the $300 Series 9 while saving you $150+. Budget shoppers should not overlook the Pro 1000, which remains one of the best values in oral care.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which Oral-B electric toothbrush is the best? +

The Oral-B iO Series 9 is the best overall, offering AI-powered tracking, 7 cleaning modes, and the most advanced micro-vibration technology. For most people, the iO Series 5 offers the best value with essential premium features at a more reasonable price.

What is the difference between Oral-B iO models? +

All iO models use the same core micro-vibration motor and brush head technology. The differences are in cleaning modes (4-7), display type (B&W vs color), AI tracking (Series 7 and 9 only), and included accessories. Cleaning performance is identical across all iO models.

Is Oral-B better than Sonicare? +

Both are excellent. Oral-B uses oscillating-rotating action that targets individual teeth with a round brush head, while Sonicare uses sonic vibrations with an elongated head for broader coverage. Clinical studies show comparable results. Personal preference for the brushing sensation often determines the best choice.
