Oral-B iO Series 9 Review: The Ultimate Flagship

Seven cleaning modes, AI-powered 3D tracking across 16 mouth zones, a gorgeous color OLED display, 14-day battery, and a premium magnetic travel case deliver the complete package. The only question is whether you need all of this or whether the Series 5 at $119 meets your needs.

Why the Series 9 Is the Best Oral-B

The Oral-B iO Series 9 is the culmination of everything Oral-B has learned about electric toothbrush design. It combines the revolutionary linear magnetic drive with the most comprehensive feature set in the lineup, wrapped in a premium build that feels luxurious in hand. It is the toothbrush for people who want no compromises.

At its core, the Series 9 uses the same linear magnetic drive as every iO model — meaning the fundamental cleaning performance is shared across the lineup. Where the Series 9 distinguishes itself is in the layered intelligence and premium experience built on top of that cleaning foundation. The AI 3D tracking, the interactive color display, the seven modes, and the build quality combine to create a brushing experience that genuinely encourages better oral hygiene habits.

AI 3D Tracking: The Signature Feature

The standout feature of the Series 9 is its AI-powered 3D tracking system. Using the Oral-B app, the toothbrush's built-in motion sensors and accelerometer work with AI algorithms to map your mouth into 16 distinct zones. As you brush, the app shows a real-time 3D model of your teeth, filling in each zone as you clean it.

This is more granular than the basic zone tracking on the Series 7 and 8, which divide your mouth into broader areas. The 3D system can distinguish between the outer, inner, and chewing surfaces of each quadrant, ensuring you give adequate attention to all surfaces. After each session, you receive a coverage score showing exactly which zones you brushed thoroughly and which you rushed through or missed entirely.

Over time, the app builds a pattern of your brushing habits, identifying consistent weak spots. This data-driven approach to brushing is genuinely useful — studies suggest that most people habitually under-brush certain areas, and having visual feedback corrects those patterns.

Color OLED Interactive Display

The Series 9's color OLED display is not just decorative — it serves as an interactive coaching tool. During brushing, the display shows:

  • Selected mode with a color-coded icon
  • Real-time timer with a visual countdown ring
  • Pressure feedback — a colored ring that shifts from green (ideal) to yellow (caution) to red (too hard)
  • Session complete animation — a smiley face when you finish 2 minutes
  • Battery level when idle

The display is bright enough to see clearly in a bathroom mirror, even in low light. The animations are smooth and responsive. It may seem like a luxury feature, but the real-time pressure feedback displayed prominently on the handle is more effective than a small LED light that you might not notice while focused on the mirror.

Seven Cleaning Modes

The Series 9 offers the most comprehensive mode selection in the Oral-B lineup:

  • Daily Clean — The everyday standard mode
  • Intense Clean — Higher intensity for a thorough deep clean
  • Whiten — Polishing pattern to reduce surface stains
  • Sensitive — Gentle mode for sensitive teeth and gums
  • Super Sensitive — The gentlest setting, ideal for post-procedure care
  • Tongue Clean — Optimized for tongue surface cleaning
  • Gum Care — A focused mode that alternates between gentle stimulation and cleaning for gum health

The Gum Care mode is exclusive to the Series 9 and Series 10 — it is not available on any lower model. For people with gingivitis or those focused on gum health, this dedicated mode provides a differentiated brushing pattern specifically designed to gently massage and clean along the gumline without aggravating sensitive tissue.

Premium Travel Case

The included travel case is a step above what lower models offer. The Series 9's premium magnetic travel case snaps shut with satisfying magnets, holds the brush handle and two replacement heads, and has a refined interior with soft-touch lining. It does not charge the brush (for that, you need the charger at home), but the 14-day battery life means most trips will not require mid-trip charging.

Battery Life

At approximately 14 days with twice-daily 2-minute sessions, the Series 9 has the longest battery life in the iO lineup (tied with the Series 10). This is a full week more than the Series 2 and 3, and four days more than the Series 5-7. For travelers, this means international trips are easily covered on a single charge. The magnetic charger reaches full charge in about 3 hours.
